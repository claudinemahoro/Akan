Akan name generator

Welcome to Akan name generator! Akan names are Ghanian days' names given to children born there according to their gender and the days on which they were born.
In this application, you are required to enter your birth year, month and date, and you choose your gender, then we generate your Akan name.Here are the Akan names:

Male names

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
